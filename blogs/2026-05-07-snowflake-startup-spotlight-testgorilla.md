As AI tools become more powerful and more commonly used, traditional recruitment processes now pose a problem for candidates and companies alike. How can recruiters find the best candidates when they have to sift through thousands of potentially AI-written CVs? And how can candidates showcase their aptitude and skills to employers, rather than being judged solely on their background? We spoke with Raul Poblador, Head of Data & AI at TestGorilla, to learn how the company aims to solve both problems by combining the latest recruitment research, data and AI technologies at scale.
