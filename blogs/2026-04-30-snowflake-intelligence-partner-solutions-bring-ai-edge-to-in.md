<p>That's where Snowflake Intelligence changes the equation. It gives teams a way to ask complex business questions in natural language and get grounded, traceable answers that span structured and unstructured data. And beyond the simple chat interface, this enterprise intelligence agent works in the background to parse queries, better identify the right organizational data and tools, and rigorously vet the accuracy of outputs. But technology alone isn't the story.</p>
<p>Across industries, Snowflake partners are showing what's possible when deep domain expertise meets enterprise intelligence. They're building industry-ready agents, departmental intelligence workflows and fast-start accelerators that help organizations move from scattered data to clear, actionable insight — securely and at scale.</p>
<p>These examples are just the beginning. They demonstrate how businesses across industries are moving beyond dashboards, accelerating decision-making and putting trusted insights into the hands of more people — all powered by Snowflake Intelligence.</p>
